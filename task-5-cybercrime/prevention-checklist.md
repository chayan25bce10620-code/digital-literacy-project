# Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist for College Students

## General Online Safety
1. Use strong, unique passwords for every account and store them in a password manager.
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts :- email, banking, social media.
3. Never click links received via SMS or WhatsApp from unknown numbers, even if they look official.
4. Verify website URLs before entering any login or payment details, look for https:// and check for spelling variations.
5. Do not share OTPs with anyone, including people claiming to be from your bank or TRAI.
6. Regularly review app permissions on your phone and revoke access for apps that don't need it.

## UPI & Financial Safety (Specific)
7. Remember: you never need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money. If someone asks you to, it is a scam.
8. Always check whether a UPI request is a "Pay" or "Collect" request before approving. Collect requests debit your account.

## If Something Goes Wrong
9. Report cybercrime immediately at cybercrime.gov.in or call Helpline 1930 (available 24x7).
10. Inform your bank within 24 hours of an unauthorised transaction to maximise chances of recovery.
