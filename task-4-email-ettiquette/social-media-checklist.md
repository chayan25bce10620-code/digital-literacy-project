# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## ✅ Do's
1. Keep your LinkedIn profile updated with academic achievements and projects.
2. Use privacy settings to control who can see your personal posts.
3. Think before you post — ask yourself if you'd be comfortable with a 
   future employer seeing it.
4. Give credit when sharing someone else's work or ideas.
5. Use social media to network meaningfully — follow professors, 
   industry professionals, and peers in your field.

## ❌ Don'ts
1. Don't share personal information like your address, phone number, 
   or daily schedule publicly.
2. Don't post or engage with content that is offensive, discriminatory, 
   or politically inflammatory.
3. Don't vent about college, professors, or classmates online — 
   it reflects poorly and spreads quickly.
4. Don't use the same password across multiple social platforms.
5. Don't accept follow/friend requests from unknown accounts, 
   especially those with no profile picture or activity.
