## Project Overview
This repository contains my Digital Literacy Portfolio, completed as 
part of the CSE0001 course project. It covers five modules — 
infographic design, digital portfolio setup, platform exploration, 
email etiquette, and cybercrime awareness.

---

## Module Summaries

**Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic**
Created a one-page infographic on Canva covering digital literacy, 
safe internet practices, and email etiquette.

**Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio**
Set up profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle with basic academic 
information and a GitHub profile README.

**Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms**
Completed the "Solve Me First" challenge on HackerRank and built a 
5-question Digital Literacy Quiz on Google Forms.

**Task 4 – Email Etiquette**
Drafted two professional emails and created a social media do's and 
don'ts checklist.

**Task 5 – Cybercrime Case Study**
Researched UPI payment fraud and produced a case study and a 
10-point prevention checklist.
